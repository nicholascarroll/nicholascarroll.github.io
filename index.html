OFX Plugins

ChannelMath, INK Keyer

Here are a couple of video compositing plugins for applications that support the OFX plugin standard, including Natron, The Foundry's Nuke, Blackmagic Fusion and Resolve, Sony Vegas Pro, SGO Mistika and SCRATCH by Assimilate.

Download

Download the plugins

ChannelMath

ChannelMath is an artist-friendly channel math expression node. It is designed to be simple and easy to use.

INK Keyer

INK is a general purpose blue/green screen keyer that is suitable for both novice and expert compositors. For the novice, the user interface is simple and self explanatory. For the expert, INK provides conveniences to make it fast to construct modular node trees.
